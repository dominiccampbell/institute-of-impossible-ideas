Working with 11 councils across the UK

We wanted to test this, by asking this question: Can local authorities work together to design greenfield, full-stack, ethical, scalable ventures that are better than current services, and cost less?

We worked with local authorities and social entrepreneurs to understand the problem and design ventures that can save money in the short term and transform the system in the long term.

Over the past three months, we’ve helped local authorities and NHS partners with their immediate response to the COVID-19 crisis. As we begin to understand the longer term implications of the pandemic, we wanted to share what we’ve been doing and what we’ve learnt.
